DTCs

DTCs are diagnostic trouble codes. These DTCs are usually set by a controller when a fault occurs due to a parameter being out of range, sensor being disconnected, etc. DTCs are requested from the controller and timestamped by the addon. The latest 10 codes are kept in the DTC channels. The # of DTCs channel allows the user to see the total number of DTCs received. The DTC byte length and byte order are needed to be able to convert the DTCs to fault codes.
